# MAA KI RASOI - Product Requirements Document

## 1. Executive Summary
### 1.1 Vision
To become India's most trusted meal subscription brand — the platform that working professionals, students, and families associate with the reliability, nutrition, and emotional comfort of home-cooked food, delivered consistently at scale.

### 1.2 Mission
Maa Ki Rasoi exists to solve a fundamental daily problem: millions of urban Indians — separated from family by ambition, migration, or circumstance — eat poorly not by choice but by necessity. Our mission is to restore the dignity of a proper meal to every urban household that deserves it.

## 5. Product Vision
### 5.1 Core Philosophy
Maa Ki Rasoi is a subscription-first, operationally-led business. 

### 5.2 Subscription-First Model
Every product decision is oriented around the subscription relationship, not the individual transaction.

## 6. Core Product Modules (MVP)
### 6.1 Subscription Management System
User Flow
- Customer selects plan type (weekly / monthly), meal type (lunch / dinner / both), and dietary preference (veg / non-veg)
- Customer selects delivery time window (12:00–1:30 PM for lunch; 7:30–9:00 PM for dinner)
- Customer completes payment via UPI or card
- Customer receives onboarding message

## 14. MVP Roadmap (90–180 Days)
### Phase 1: Launch — Days 1–60
Objective: Controlled pilot with 80–120 subscribers in 1 cluster; validate operations, menu, and delivery model.

**Week 3–4**: PWA (basic): Subscription signup, plan selection, UPI payment integration (Razorpay); no app — mobile web only
**Week 5–6**: Kitchen Operations Dashboard v1 (spreadsheet-based is acceptable); menu configured for 4-week rotation
