# Maa Ki Rasoi – Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

## 1. Introduction and Definitions
Maa Ki Rasoi is a hyperlocal subscription-based tiffin delivery service operating in Delhi with a single central kitchen and an in-house delivery team. 

- **Customer:** Subscriber receiving meals via monthly/weekly plan.
- **Subscription:** Prepaid meal plan (e.g., 25 meals/month) billed weekly or monthly.
- **Order:** Daily or weekly scheduled meals per customer’s plan.
- **Kitchen SOP:** Documented steps for meal prep and hygiene.
- **Delivery Partner:** In-house riders or hired personnel who deliver meals.
- **SLA (Service Level Agreement):** Committed performance targets (e.g. on-time delivery rate).

## 2. Operating Principles
- **Quality and Freshness:** All meals must be prepared fresh daily, prioritizing local, home-style taste and nutrition.
- **Hygiene and Safety:** Maintain strict food safety compliance (personal hygiene, sanitation) as mandated by FSSAI. Use PPE (gloves, masks) and frequent handwashing.
- **Customer Focus:** Be polite, responsive, and solution-oriented. Use data (feedback, NPS) to guide improvements.
- **Consistency:** Deliver consistent taste, portions, and timely service. Ensure recipes and portions are standardized.
- **Transparency:** Clearly communicate menu, pricing, and policies. Display required licenses and comply with billing regulations.

## 3. Daily Operations Overview (Kitchen to Delivery)
- **Pre-Shipment Checks:** Review the day’s subscription orders each evening. Confirm menu selections, dietary restrictions, and pack counts. Prepare a dispatch manifest.
- **Morning Prep:** Early morning, clean and sanitize all workstations and equipment. Perform inventory checks (FIFO/FEFO). Pre-cook staples and prep vegetables. Monitor cooking temperatures (hot >60°C, cold <5°C).
- **Portioning and Packaging:** Portion each meal according to standard measures. Label with contents, date, and customer name. 
- **Quality Inspection:** Randomly taste-test samples. Ensure packaging is sealed properly.
- **Dispatch:** Load meals into insulated delivery bags. Use route optimization (cluster deliveries by area).
- **Delivery:** Riders depart by mid-morning (aim for all by lunchtime). Ensure contactless hand-off or customer signature. Record issues.
- **Post-Delivery:** Log feedback/complaints. Begin kitchen clean-up. Record daily logs (orders completed, incidents, inventory used).

## 4. Subscription Management Protocol
- **Signups and Renewals:** Accept via app or WhatsApp. Collect name, address, meal preferences, payment method. Auto-renew monthly by default.
- **Billing:** Generate invoices. Use digital payments or post-dated cheques. Send reminders. Only activate meals after payment confirmation.
- **Changes and Pauses:** Allow plan updates at least 3 days before next cycle. Permit short pauses notified before the cutoff.
- **Cancellations:** Process immediately for future deliveries. Offer prorated refunds/credits if mid-cycle. Track churn.
- **Accounting:** Reconcile weekly. Maintain a ledger of active subscribers and payments.

## 5. Kitchen SOP (Preparation, Hygiene, Portioning)
- **Personal Hygiene:** Wash hands before cooking, after breaks. Wear clean uniforms, hairnets, and gloves. No jewelry.
- **Ingredient Prep:** Wash all raw produce in potable water. Thaw items in refrigerator (≤5°C) or microwave.
- **Raw vs Cooked Separation:** Use separate cutting boards. Segregate veg vs non-veg prep.
- **Temperature Control:** Keep hot items >60°C, cold items <5°C. Do not leave perishable foods at room temp >2 hours.
- **No Bare-Hands:** Handle ready-to-eat food only with sanitized utensils or gloved hands.
- **Portioning:** Use calibrated scales and standardized cards.
- **Sanitation:** Clean and sanitize after use. "Clean as you go" policy. Air dry utensils.
- **Waste Disposal:** Bins with covers. Prompt disposal. Segregation (wet vs dry).

## 6. Procurement & Inventory Process
- **Vendor Selection:** Vetted suppliers with valid licenses. Maintain backups.
- **Ordering:** Forecast based on peak-week consumption. Set reorder points.
- **Receipt & Inspection:** Verify packaging, expiry, and quality. Reject if damaged.
- **Storage:** Dry goods ventilated and 6 inches off floor. Refrigerate perishables ≤5°C. Label with dates. Follow FIFO/FEFO.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Digital logs (daily update) + weekly physical count.
- **Storage Hygiene:** Pest-free and dry. Check temperatures daily. Log wastage.

## 7. Quality Assurance Checklist
- **Kitchen Sanitation:** Daily verification of surfaces/counters (FSSAI standard).
- **Food Safety Checks:** Temperature monitoring of hot/cold batches.
- **Utensils and Storage:** Condition check (no cracks/chips). raw/cooked segregation.
- **Packaging Inspection:** Sturdiness and labeling verification.
- **Portion Accuracy:** Sampling for weight consistency.
- **Dispatch Readiness:** Manifest cross-check.
- **End-of-Day Review:** Incident logging and corrective action notes.

## 8. Menu Management & Rotation
- **Variety & Balance:** 4-week rotating cycle. Staples + protein + extras.
- **Seasonal Adjustments:** Seasonal availability (e.g., mango in summer).
- **Dietary Options:** Customization (low-carb, light, Jain, etc.).
- **Customer Feedback:** Retire unpopular items based on complaints/NPS.
- **Special Occasions:** Festive/themed specials communicated in advance.

## 9. Order and Delivery Routing Flow
- **Order Processing:** Consolidate confirmed orders with unique IDs.
- **Clustering:** Group by geographic zone/building cluster.
- **Route Planning:** Optimize routes via GPS/Map tools.
- **Assign Riders:** Load balance per route. Provide route sheets.
- **Real-time Tracking:** Monitor progress via mobile app.
- **Proof of Delivery (POD):** Signature, photo, or OTP/WhatsApp acknowledgment.
- **Post-Delivery Closure:** Update status. Flag non-deliveries for support.

## 10. Delivery Partner SOP
- **Preparation:** Arrive by dispatch time. Verify load. Insulated bag check.
- **Hygiene:** Clean uniforms, face masks, sanitized hands.
- **Food Handling:** Tamper-evident seals. Do not open packages.
- **Temperature Control:** Maintain >60°C for hot, <5°C for cold during transit.
- **Vehicle/Bag Maintenance:** Daily cleaning and inspection.
- **Delivery Protocol:** Doorbell/knock confirm. 5-minute wait time. Return undelivered.
- **Documentation:** Mark completed. Log exceptions/delays.
- **Safety:** No non-food items in the bag. Store chemicals separately.

## 11. Customer Support Playbook
- **Channels:** Phone, WhatsApp Business, Email. <2 hour response time.
- **Tone:** Courteous, empathetic, positive language.
- **Information Gathering:** Order details + identity verification.
- **Issue Resolution:** Prepared templates for common issues. Prompt follow-up.
- **Escalation:** Immediate escalation for food safety or unresolved issues.
- **Documentation:** Log every interaction in CRM.
- **After-Call/Chat:** Confirm next steps and timeline.

## 12. Complaint Resolution Workflow
- **Listen carefully:** Notes on all details.
- **Acknowledge & Apologize:** Empathize without blaming.
- **Resolve Collaboration:** Propose immediate remedies (refund, replaced).
- **Confirm Resolution:** Verify customer satisfaction with the solution.
- **Thank the Customer:** Rebuild goodwill.
- **Follow-Up:** verify solution delivery. Log for trend review.

## 13. WhatsApp & App Coordination Rules
- **WhatsApp Business:** Official account only. Use templates.
- **Automation:** Greetings and away messages.
- **Quick Replies:** Personalize templates with customer names.
- **Integration:** Sync chat history with CRM/Admin system.
- **Response Time:** <1 hour for inquiries.
- **Etiquette:** Opt-in only for broadcasts.
- **Record Keeping:** Log orders/requests immediately.

## 14. Admin Panel & Dashboard Update SOP
- **Features:** User management, Menu, Subscriptions, Coupons, Rider assignment, Inventory, Analytics.
- **Daily Updates:** Review metrics, inventory sync, post announcements.
- **Weekly Maintenance:** Reports (churn, ARPU), check error logs.
- **Data Integrity:** Manual adjustment reconciliations (cash sales).
- **Access Control:** Role-based access (RBAC). Strong passwords.
- **Backup:** Daily/weekly database backups.

## 15. Refund & Compensation Rules
- **Eligibility:** Verifiable failures (wrong meal, late >30 min, safety).
- **Types:** Full Refund, Partial Credit, Free Replacement, Goodwill Discount.
- **Process:** Report within 2 hours. Support verifies. Manager approves. Confirm in 24h.
- **Limits:** No refund for customer scheduling changes after cutoff.
- **Method:** Original payment method or Wallet.

## 16. Food Packaging & Labelling Standards
- **Materials:** FSSAI food-grade. No unsafe materials (newspapers).
- **Insulation:** Tertiary insulation layer for transit.
- **Portion Boxes:** Tamper-evident seals. Labeled with customer name.
- **Labels:** Product name, ingredients, net weight, date/time, expiry, FSSAI #, storage info, allergen warnings.
- **Eco-friendly:** Use sustainable materials where safe.

## 17. Compliance and Licensing (FSSAI, GST)
- **FSSAI:** Basic registration (≤12L) or State License (>12L). Annual renewal.
- **Display:** License # must be visible in kitchen/premises.
- **Training:** FoSTaC certificates for staff.
- **GST:** Mandatory if >20L turnover. 5% GST (no ITC) for meal delivery.
- **Records:** Maintain GST invoices and returns. Fire safety compliance.

## 18. Shift Timings & Staff Responsibilities
- **Kitchen:** 5:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Prep/Pack).
- **Delivery:** 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Riders).
- **Sanitation:** 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM (Deep clean).
- **Management:** 5:00 AM – 4:00 PM (Escalations).
- **Attendance:** Mandatory clock-in/out. Late arrival logging.

## 19. Payroll & Incentives Tracking
- **Wages:** Local minimum wage + Overtime (2x). Pay by 5th of month.
- **Incentives:** Bonus for punctuality, low complaints, 98% on-time delivery.
- **Records:** Digital register with components and deductions.
- **Cash Flow:** Buffer fund for payroll security.

## 20. Vendor Management Guidelines
- **Selection:** Quality consistency and safety certs. Multiple sources for milk/meat.
- **Agreements:** Fixed pricing for staples. Copy of licenses on file.
- **Tracking:** Monthly rating on Quality, Timeliness, Compliance.
- **Inventory:** Reorder points for packaging/condiments.

## 21. Safety and Incident Protocols
- **Kitchen Safety:** Fire extinguisher, first-aid, emergency contacts on wall.
- **Injuries:** Log in Accident Register. Immediate first aid.
- **Rider Incidents:** Call emergency services. Report to owner.
- **Food Recall:** Halt related meals, identify customers, dispose safely.
- **Hygiene Logs:** Handwashing and cleaning schedules.

## 22. SLA Commitments & Penalties
- **Delivery SLA:** Goal: 1:00 PM. Target: 95% on-time.
- **Accuracy SLA:** 99% order accuracy.
- **Support SLA:** 2-hour response, 4-hour max.
- **Penalties:** Internal performance points system for staff/suppliers.

## 23. Backup & Contingency Plans
- **Outages:** Backup LPG, Inverter, ready-to-cook stock.
- **Shortage:** Alternative ingredients or proactive customer notice.
- **Breakdown:** Spare burners, knives, bags, and phones.
- **Absence:** designated backups and cross-trained staff.
- **IT Failure:** Paper manifest backup + Cloud sync later.

## 24. Monthly Reporting Template (Core KPIs)
- Total Subscriptions / New Subscribers / Churn Rate.
- Total Orders Served / On-Time % / Accuracy %.
- ARPU (Avg Revenue Per User) / Total Revenue.
- Food Cost % (COGS).
- Complaints Resolved / NPS Score.

## 25. NPS / Feedback Collection Process
- **Method:** Quarterly survey ("How likely to recommend 0-10?").
- **Scoring:** promoters (9-10), passives (7-8), detractors (0-6).
- **Formula:** %Promoters - %Detractors.
- **Goal:** NPS > 50 (Excellent). Use comments for pain-point detection.

## 26. Onboarding for New Team Members
- Orientation, hands-on pairing for 2 days, shadowing, then supervised tasks.
- Onboarding checklist (Uniform, safety training, FSSAI basics).
- Customer support role-play for agents.

## 27. Growth Playbook (Cluster Expansion)
- Trigger: >100 customers in a cluster + 80% rider capacity utilized.
- Pilot Runs: referral-only launch in adjacent zones.
- Cluster Replication: Copy successful team structures.

## 28. Data Logging Standards (AI Readiness)
- Log all events with Timestamps: OrderPlaced, Cooked, Dispatched, Delivered.
- Incident/QA database tagged with OrderID and StaffID.
- Structured Export: CSV/JSON formats for analytical ingestion.

- Payments: Razorpay/Paytm.
- Ops: WhatsApp Business API / Google Maps API.
- Maintenance: GIT Version control, Daily DB Backups.
